About

I make brands look the part on Shopify.

I'm Adel — a Shopify developer who builds custom themes and storefronts that feel premium and convert.

I work directly in Liquid and the Online Store 2.0 architecture — JSON templates, modular sections and blocks, theme settings, and metafields — so store owners can edit everything from the theme editor without breaking anything. I've built menswear, supplements, and lifestyle brands, plus conversion-focused landing pages for healthcare clients.

Beyond themes, I add the things that actually move revenue: upsells and "frequently bought together", cart drawers, swatches, sticky add-to-cart, fast loading, and clean mobile UX — in Arabic (RTL) and English.
